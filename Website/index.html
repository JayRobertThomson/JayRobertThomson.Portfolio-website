About me

Information About Me

Born in Johannesburg, South Africa. I now live in Germany, married to my Swiss/German wife with our two kids. After spending 15 years as a professional cyclist, racing the roads of the world, which includes racing the biggest races like the Tour de France. In my time as a professional cyclist I have used many of my soft skills such as communication, motivating myself to be the best I can be, being flexible, being responsible, Networking with sponsors and the community, and finally but far from the last was team building. Alongside my career as a cyclist, I have studied business and management at the University of Essex. Now after many years of analyzing my own training data, I have now turned to Data analytics. Furthermore, I have gained experience as a customer service manager where I have studied the customer service data looking for trends in customer behavior. Now having changed focus with my current company into a position as marketing manager, with a focus on Public Relations and Athlete sponsorship. However, I still have the responsibility of analyzing the customer support data to find deeper insights into customer behavior. Data has been always been part of my life for as long as I can remember, as I analyzed my own training/race data to find patterns in finding better shape to improve my own performance. CareerFoundry has given me the tools to take this love for data to the next level.
